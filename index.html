Research Interests

My interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and AI security, with a particular focus on building systems resilient to adversarial attacks. I have led studies investigating Unicode perturbation detection, AI-optimized case selection for content moderation, and adversarial text-to-image prompt mitigation. My work reflects a personal commitment to leveraging technology to create a safer, more equitable world.
